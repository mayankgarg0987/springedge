Transactional Messaging


SpringEdge's messaging rest APIs can be directly integrate into software, Web based applications and modules to meet many common
business communication requirements, and customized solutions.

According to TRIA regulations, messages sent by commercial entities are classified imto Promotional and Transactional messages.
Messages sent through Transactional SMS gateway service will get delivered to do not disturb or NDNC registered customers also.
Though the sms is not of promotional nature, sometimes it becomes necessary to send out an alert or service messages to users.

While account sign-up, choose a 6 alphabet Sender Id to send transactional SMS from. It is best to choose send name similar to you
business name so customers can easily identify your business, Also it help business to build a brand name. All messages will be sent
using this Sender name from transactional sms gateway using Transactional SMS API and SMS platform

2-way Messaging


Spring Edge offers you a 2 - Way Messaging platform which allows you to receive SMS from customer and to interact with your mobile
audience, to stimulate consumer interest or collect consumer information through advertising or marketing campaigns. It allows
subscribers to respond to sms campaigns, send follow-up replies, provide feedback, participate in loyalty programs and competitions as
well as build new and existing relationships.

To run a 2-way messaging service, a number is required to receive the inbound message. We offers short codes and long numbers to
receive real-time SMS responses immediately and efficiently. These ten digit numbers are accessible globally and not just restricted
among networks in India. Subscribers can send sms from anywhere to virtual number and that message can directly visible on web, You
can have a further execution like sending a confirmation or response based on your requirement. Moreover, messages on long codes are
charged as per cost of regular SMS plan of the subscriber unlike sms on short codes which are charged at a premium rate. Long code is
an apt solution to strike a two way communication with existing customers hence resolving their queries and even intimating them about
any notification.

With our trusted reliable gateway and cost-effective reply paths, the message replies can be forwarded to email addresses, stored in the
application Inbox, or forwarded to your URL via our API.
